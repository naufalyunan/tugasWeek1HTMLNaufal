What is a film?



Film is a thin flexible strip of plastic or other material coated with light-sensitive emulsion for exposure in a camera, used to produce photographs or motion pictures. Nowadays, it is often used to replicate a vintage photo to express the shooter perspective and originality because one could never know the result before it is developed.
